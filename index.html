I'm Vukasin aka Lomz, explorer focusing on the intersection of art and technology.

With a background in creative photography and years of experience in product design, NFTs, and multimedia art, I like to blur the lines between visual storytelling and digital innovation. From building crypto wallets and NFT platforms to crafting generative art on fx(hash), my work spans across design systems, experimental code, and sound.

I'm currently designing at Peer, and before that, I helped shape products at DeFi Saver and XDEFI Wallet.
