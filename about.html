Art × Technology.

I'm Vukasin (Lomz) — a product designer and multimedia artist working at the intersection of art and technology.

By day I'm a product designer at Peer — UX/UI, design systems, prototyping. By night I build generative art on fx(hash), Arduino synths, drawing machines, dithering tools and small interactive things. The two halves feed each other: the obsessive way I explore new tools for personal work is what helps me bring AI 3D, procedural generation and game-engine prototypes back into the product. Beyond the Pixels goes deeper on that idea.

I studied creative photography at the University of Applied Arts in Belgrade, and have spent the past decade shipping digital products — crypto wallets, NFT platforms, DeFi tools — while keeping the art practice running in parallel. Two halves of the same desk.
